FBLA or Future Business Leaders of America is an organization that is focused on making young adults into leaders, most of the events held by FBLA are competitions that are sponsored by Large Corporations. FBLA is associated with the West-Mec coding program, meaning that you can get a job if a corporation notices you at one of said competitions. FBLA is a great way to get jobs, scholarships, and many other opportunities. FBLA was founded in 1946 by Hamden L. Forkner, and FBLA continued on to sponsor many schools, charters, and events from then on. FBLA is a club that focuses on creating young business leaders.
