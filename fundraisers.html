In order to fund the trips for the Coding program, the Coding Program needs make money through many opportunities that show up. Such as the current Fundraisers that include the shoe drive and Sonic Community Coupon Cards. The Sonic Community Cards are sold for 5$ and include many different coupons. The coding program then donates these shoes to a company that than recycles the materials to make new shoes.
